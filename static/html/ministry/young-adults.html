Young Adults

Young Adult Ministry is an outreach of the Catholic Church in the parish setting to young adults. Here at Our Lady, Mother of the Church, we aim to connect young women and men in their twenties and thirties, married and single in order to discern their faith according to their experience and understanding of life in a culture of challenges. In our journey, we discover who we are as Catholics and find our true identity in Christ. Our purpose is to put people in contact with others their own age who share their faith and values, and offer some alternatives to what is currently available in our culture.

The young adult ministry offers a wide variety of opportunities to grow and develop one’s relationship with God through a personal encounter with one another. Through this encounter, we build a support group that may help us nurture each other’s faith-experience through worship, sharing, inputs, workshops, ministry and even sharing a meal together.

We meet every 2nd and 4th Saturday of each month at the Hayes parish hall beginning September 12, 2015. If you have further inquiries regarding the young adult ministry program, Please contact Dr. Glenn Bisquera at 773.625.2273 or email at dre.olmc@gmail.com.
